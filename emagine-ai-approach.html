Our AI Approach

Training, transformation, and technical solutions — mapped to the customer lifecycle

For discussion Workshop March 27
We don't just train people on AI or advise on transformation. We give organizations a complete AI implementation approach — from discovering problems to embedding solutions that stick.

The Problem

Four patterns we see in nearly every organization. This is why standalone training or standalone consulting isn't enough — and why we need a coherent approach that connects training, transformation, technology, and governance.

📉
Training fades
70-90% of training content is lost within 30 days. Without persistent systems that reinforce learning, every investment in AI skills evaporates.
🔄
Chat-based AI is a dead end
Every AI conversation starts from zero. No memory, no compounding. Employees repeat the same prompts, the same mistakes, the same discovery work — forever.
🚪
Knowledge walks out the door
When people leave, their AI know-how goes with them. No persistent systems capture what was learned, decided, or built. The organization starts over.
🧪
AI stays isolated
Pilots everywhere, nothing scales. Every team runs its own experiment. No governance, no shared patterns, no path from experiment to organizational capability.

This section is NOT on the slides — it's for the verbal conversation with Christian (via Teams). The idea: every individual gets a persistent AI assistant (personal brain), teams share knowledge through a team brain, and the organization governs it all through a platform like Ignition.
